Hi. I'm Ana Iova.

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science of the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science , University of Bucharest

My research interests include model based software testing using evolutionary approaches, testing Cyber-Physical systems and membrane Computing
